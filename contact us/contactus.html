Why Choose us

  • Quality Workmanship
  • Personal Touch
  • Fresh Products
  • Value
  • Experience


Quality Workmanship- Years of working for other great decorators who had fabulous work ethics and high standards for cake quality instilled the attention to detail that I have.  I am well versed in buttercream as well as fondant work.  Not all decorators are - so when you shop around it's best to ask. My portfolio shows both buttercream and fondant cakes.

Personal Touch - ​When you come to me for a cake I am the one who will talk with you at your consultation, the one who will work up a custom design, bake your cake, decorate it and deliver it.  I do not farm out any portions of my business to others.  I focus on quality.  I take great pride in each and every cake I make.  From a 6 tier wedding cake to a simple birthday cake. Every customer deserves the same quality - and I make sure I deliver on that!

Fresh Products - I bake each and every cake to order.  I do not bake weeks ahead and pull out what I need from a stockpile of frozen cakes.  My cakes are are made from tried & true recipes. I use the freshest ingredients to make the best cake possible.   My cakes are chilled to help with ease of assembling, icing and transport.



Value - ​Before you select a baker/decorator it is best to check and compare prices.  I have found that my prices compare nicely to some of the larger bakeries in the Dallas/Ft. Worth area especially considering the quality, care and service that I provide.  Quality cakes are works of art and take time.  I'm sure you've heard the old saying "cheap cake isn't good and good cake isn't cheap".  There is no way I could ever compete with grocery store or warehouse store pricing but the proof is in the end result. I may not be the cheapest cake quote you get but are you looking for cheap? or are you looking for amazing?

Experience - I have been decorating cakes for over 10 years.  I have attended culinary school and learned much of what I know on the job also.  I am constantly learning new techniques and keeping up with the latest trends for wedding cakes and general cake design
